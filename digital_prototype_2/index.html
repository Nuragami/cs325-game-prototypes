Revenge of the Organ Donors

  • High Concept: Collect as many organs as you can while surviving the hoards of zombies.
  • Theme: The theme is a mix of comical horror and
  • Mandated Variety: The mandated varieties are keyboard for input, a lot of randomness for randomness, collector and survival for playstyle/genre.
  • Player Experience Goals: The primary goal of this game is to test the player's ability to survive under preasure. Players must precisely navigate the level and avoid the enemies.
  • Gameplay: Players control the doctor's movement with WASD keys. Three different type of organs will spawn at random locations in the level. As the player collects more organs, more organs will spawn. The amount of organs collected will determine the number of enemies that are also spawned. The more the player collects, the more difficult the game gets. Players must avoid the enemies and can not fight back. The objective is to collect as many organs before the player eventually dies.
  • Prototype Goal: The prototype is evaulating the random spawn of items in world space. It is also evaulating the enemy spawn curve as player collects more items.
  • Features: The unique feature of this game is the art as well as the comical feel to the horror genre.
  • Setting/Premise: The game takes place in the middle of a random road. The doctor must collect the organs that are spawned on the road, while the owner of the organs come back to take revenge.
  • Story: There was a tragic accident on a road one day. Many of the victims were organ donors. A doctor was set out to collect the victims' organs. However, as the doctor harvested the organs; the owners' of those organs came back to life to take revenge on the doctor.
  • Target Audience: The game is rated PG for comical volience and gore.
  • Play Time: The game playtime can last anywhere between 30seconds to 30 minutes.
  • Strategies: Carefully navigate the level to avoid the zombies. Use existing items as barriers.
